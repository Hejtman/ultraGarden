Function

2 weeks self-sustaining aero/hydroponics system for extreme sunbeam. Ultra sound made nutrient fog circling in pipes on balcony trough plants roots. 2 liters of liquid water are pumped every quarter hour for fully grown plants with roots reaching bottom of pipe via aquarium watter pump.

Raspberry Pi collects information (light density, humidity, temperature, watter level and manages fogging, watering, airing/cooling cycles for optimal plants irrigation.

Watering

Weakly consumption:

  • 10ml hydrogen peroxide - mildew prevention, root aeration
  • 5ml Nitric acid - pH reduction 7 >> 6, root aeration
  • 2 banana peelings as fertilizer
